Overview

MON is a fast-paced roguelike prototype focused on reactive combat systems. The goal is to create gameplay where players must constantly adapt and react to enemy behavior instead of relying on memorization.

Gameplay

The combat system is designed around timing, positioning and quick decision-making. Each encounter pushes the player to stay alert and respond instantly to threats.

Design Decisions

⚔️ Reactive Combat

The combat was designed to prioritize reaction over memorization. Enemies require the player to adapt in real time, creating tension and engagement.

🎯 Player Feedback

Strong visual feedback was implemented to ensure that every action feels impactful and readable during fast-paced gameplay.

⚖️ Difficulty Balance

The difficulty curve was tuned to feel challenging but fair, encouraging improvement rather than frustration.

What I Learned

  • Designing responsive combat systems
  • Iterating quickly through playtesting
  • Balancing difficulty and player feedback
